Some of the Top Anime Series that got delayed due to the effects of Covid-19:

Sword Art Online: Alicization - War of Underworld

The official website for the Sword Art Online: Alicization anime announced on Friday that the  Sword Art Online: Alicization War of Underworld Part 2 anime is postponed to July due to effects of the spread of the COVID-19 coronavirus illness in Japan.

A Certain Scientific Railgun T

The official website for the A Certain Scientific Railgun T (Toaru Kagaku no Railgun T) anime announced on Saturday that the anime's 13th episode is delayed due to the new coronavirus disease (COVID-19) situation. The episode was scheduled to air on April 24, but the 12th episode will re-air instead on that date.The staff will reveal later when the 13th episode will air.

Re:Zero Season 2

The official website of the Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- anime revealed on Monday that the anime's second season is delayed from April to July. The staff explained that "the global issues of the COVID-19 coronavirus illness have had a big effect on the production" of the show.The staff will reveal the new start date, time, and channels at a later date.
